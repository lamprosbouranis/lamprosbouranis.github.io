Research interests

  • Bayesian statistics – model selection; evidence/marginal likelihood estimation.
  • Intractable likelihoods.
  • Statistical network analysis: Bayesian inference for statistical network models and applications in social network analysis.
  • Composite likelihood inference – applications to Exponential random graph models.
  • Stochastic epidemiology.
  • Software implementation.
